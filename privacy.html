Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 24, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how VEX ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: If the App requires login, we may collect your email, username, or other basic profile information.
  • User-Generated Content: Audio recordings, voice messages, text messages, or media you choose to upload.
  • Support Information: When contacting support, we may collect your messages or attachments.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, device identifiers, language settings.
  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, crash logs, diagnostics.

We do not collect sensitive personal data unless required by the app functionality and disclosed to you.

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use your information to:

  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information with:

  • Service Providers (e.g., hosting servers, analytics tools)

All third-party services comply with data protection requirements.

4. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to operate the App or comply with legal obligations.

You may request deletion of your data by contacting us at vex@ajamais.ai.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If such data is found, we delete it immediately.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures (encryption, access controls) to protect your data. However, no method of transmission is 100% secure.

Depending on your region, you may have rights such as:
