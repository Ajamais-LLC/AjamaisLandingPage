Structora Zoom Integration Guide

Overview

Connect your company's Zoom account to Structora to schedule meetings, record them, and automatically capture transcripts for AI-powered meeting notes.

Installation

  1. Open Structora and navigate to the Meetings tab
  2. Click "Schedule Meeting" and select Zoom as the meeting provider
  3. Click "Connect Zoom" — you will be redirected to Zoom
  4. Sign in with your Zoom account and authorize Structora
  5. Once connected, you will see a green "Zoom connected" badge

Permissions

Structora requests the following Zoom permissions:

  • Create meetings: Schedule Zoom meetings directly from Structora
  • Update/delete meetings: Modify or cancel scheduled meetings
  • Create invite links: Generate meeting invitation links for participants
  • Read cloud recordings: Access meeting recordings and transcripts after meetings end

What data does Structora access?

  • Meeting ID and join URL (stored for scheduling)
  • Cloud recording transcripts (stored encrypted, linked to your project)
  • Structora does NOT access your Zoom contacts, chat messages, or account settings

Using the Integration

  1. Schedule a meeting: Select Zoom as the provider, set time and participants, click Schedule
  2. Join a meeting: Click "Join" on any scheduled Zoom meeting to open it in the embedded viewer
  3. After the meeting: Click "Fetch Transcript" to pull the cloud recording transcript. AI analysis will automatically extract key decisions, action items, and risks.

Removing the Integration

  1. In the Meetings tab, select Zoom as provider and click "Disconnect"
  2. To fully remove, go to zoom.us/profile → Apps → find Structora → Remove
  3. All stored meeting data can be deleted by contacting info@ajamais.ai

Data Handling

  • Transcripts are stored encrypted in Structora's database
  • Default retention: 30 days (configurable)
  • Users can delete transcripts at any time from the platform
  • Disconnecting Zoom does not automatically delete previously stored transcripts
