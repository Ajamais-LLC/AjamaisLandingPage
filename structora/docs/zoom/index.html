Structora — Documentation

Zoom Integration

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Overview

The Structora Zoom integration brings ontology-driven structuring to your Zoom meetings. After installation, Structora can summarize, structure, and index meeting content you authorize it to process — turning conversations into reusable knowledge.

Installation

  1. Open the Zoom App Marketplace and sign in with your Zoom account.
  2. Search for Structora and open the listing.
  3. Click Add and review the permissions requested.
  4. Authorize the app. You will be redirected to Structora to complete setup.

Permissions & Scopes

Structora requests only the minimum scopes required to operate. Typical scopes include:

  • user:read — to identify the signed-in Zoom user and display account context.
  • meeting:read — to retrieve metadata for meetings you choose to structure.
  • recording:read (optional) — to import meeting transcripts or recordings when you explicitly request it.

You can review and revoke these scopes at any time from the Zoom Marketplace.

Using Structora in a Meeting

  1. Start or join a Zoom meeting as you normally would.
  2. Open the Apps panel in the Zoom client and select Structora.
  3. Sign in with your Structora account if prompted.
  4. Choose an action — Summarize, Extract Ontology, or Save to Workspace.
  5. Review the generated structure in the Structora side panel or open it in your Structora workspace after the meeting.

Data Handling

Structora only processes the content you explicitly send to it. We transmit data over TLS and store it encrypted at rest. Access is limited to authenticated users within your workspace.

For full details, see the Privacy Policy.

Uninstallation

  1. Sign in to the Zoom App Marketplace.
  2. Click Manage → Installed Apps.
  3. Find Structora and click Remove.

Upon uninstallation, OAuth tokens are revoked immediately and associated data is deleted within 30 days.

Troubleshooting

The app doesn't appear in my Zoom client

Ensure your Zoom client is updated to the latest version and that your admin has approved Structora for your organization.

Authorization failed

Sign out of Zoom and Structora, then retry the installation. If the error persists, contact us below with the error message and timestamp.

Missing data after a meeting

Structora processes only content you explicitly send. If expected content is missing, verify the action was triggered during the meeting or contact support.
