Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Àjamais LLC ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use Structora, including the Structora Zoom integration (collectively, the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information from Zoom

  • Account Information: Zoom user ID, account ID, display name, email address, and profile data shared via the Zoom OAuth scopes you authorize.
  • Meeting Metadata: Meeting ID, topic, start/end time, host, and participant identifiers for meetings where Structora is invoked.
  • Meeting Content (only when explicitly enabled): Transcripts, audio, or shared materials that you choose to send to Structora for structuring.

1.2 Information You Provide

  • Documents, notes, or ontology inputs you upload to Structora.
  • Support requests and related attachments.

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device and browser information, IP address, language settings.
  • Usage data, feature interactions, diagnostic and crash logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Operate the Structora Zoom integration and deliver its core features (summarization, structuring, ontology generation).
  • Authenticate users and maintain secure sessions.
  • Improve performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • Respond to support requests and communicate service updates.
  • Detect abuse and comply with legal obligations.

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with:

  • Service Providers that host infrastructure, process data on our behalf, or provide analytics under confidentiality obligations.
  • Zoom Video Communications, Inc., for the sole purpose of operating the integration you initiated.
  • Law Enforcement, only when legally required.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain Zoom data only as long as required to provide the Service or meet legal obligations. When you uninstall the Structora app from the Zoom Marketplace, we delete associated OAuth tokens and user data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing structora@ajamais.ai.

5. Security

We use industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical safeguards, including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest, scoped OAuth tokens, and access controls. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Structora is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, or to opt out of certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact structora@ajamais.ai.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via the Service or email. Continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
