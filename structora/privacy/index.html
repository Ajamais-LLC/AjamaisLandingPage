Structora Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Ajamais AI ("we", "our", "us") operates the Structora platform. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email address, organization name
  • Project data: project details, documents, and files you upload
  • Meeting data: meeting titles, scheduled times, participant emails, and transcripts (when you connect your Zoom account)
  • Usage data: how you interact with the platform

Zoom Integration

When you connect your Zoom account, Structora accesses:

  • Meeting creation: We create Zoom meetings on your behalf and store only the meeting ID and join URL
  • Cloud recordings: We fetch meeting transcripts after meetings end for AI-powered analysis
  • We do not access your Zoom contacts, chat messages, or account settings

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the Structora platform
  • To generate AI-powered meeting insights (summaries, action items, decisions)
  • To store project knowledge for search and retrieval

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored encrypted at rest
  • Data is transmitted over TLS/HTTPS
  • Meeting transcripts are linked to your project and can be deleted at any time

Data Retention

  • Project data is retained while your account is active
  • Meeting transcripts follow your configured retention period (default 30 days)
  • You can delete any data at any time from the platform

Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Contact us at info@ajamais.ai for any data requests
