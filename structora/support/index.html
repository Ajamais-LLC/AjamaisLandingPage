Frequently asked questions

How do I install the Structora app on Zoom?

Visit the Zoom App Marketplace, search for "Structora", click Install, and authorize the scopes requested. You will be redirected back to Structora once installation completes.

How do I uninstall the app?

Go to the Zoom App Marketplace, open the Manage Apps section, find Structora, and click Remove. Your OAuth tokens and associated data will be deleted within 30 days.

How is my Zoom data handled?

We only access the data scopes you authorize, and we use it solely to deliver the features you invoke. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I request data deletion?

Email structora@ajamais.ai from the address associated with your account and we will process the request within 30 days.
