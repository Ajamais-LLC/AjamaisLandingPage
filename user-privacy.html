Vex — Privacy

User Privacy Choices

Last updated: November 24, 2025

At VEX, we respect your privacy and give you control over your personal information. This page explains the choices you can make regarding how your data is collected and used.

1. Request to Access or Delete Your Data

You may request:

  • A copy of your personal data
  • Deletion of your account and associated data
  • Correction of inaccurate information

To make a request, contact us at:

vex@ajamais.ai

We will verify your identity before processing your request.

2. Opt Out of Analytics

You may choose to limit how we use analytics tools to improve the app's performance.

If you want to opt out of analytics tracking, contact us at:

vex@ajamais.ai

We will process your request within a reasonable timeframe.

3. Opt Out of Personalized Content

If the app uses personalization (recommendations, matching, etc.), you can request to disable such features.

To opt out, email us at:

vex@ajamais.ai

Disabling personalization may affect app functionality.

4. Revoke Microphone or Camera Access

You can disable access at any time:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Microphone / Camera → Toggle off VEX

5. Delete Account from Inside the App

If the app includes an in-app "Delete Account" button, you may use it at any time.

If not available, please email us and we will delete your data manually.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about your privacy choices, contact us at:

vex@ajamais.ai
